Overview

The live MBA page states that the course is called Two Years Full Time Master of Business Administration, abbreviated as MBA. It preserves attendance requirements, semester structure, passing rules, repeater examination rules, ACPC admission process, CMAT requirement, and syllabus links.

Duration

2 years full-time

Eligibility

Candidate must have appeared in CMAT as prescribed/conducted by AICTE for the corresponding year and must have passed a qualifying Bachelor Degree examination with minimum 50% marks under the rules stated on the live MBA page. Final-year candidates may apply subject to passing the qualifying examination with 50% marks at admission.

Admission Process

The Government of Gujarat Education Department constituted ACPC to regulate MBA admissions through online counselling, adopted by the college. CMAT is required as stated in the live page.

Attendance Rules

A student satisfies attendance requirements only if they attend not less than 75% of classes in each subject of each semester. Students not satisfying attendance are not eligible for the end semester university examination in that subject. Attendance status is provided every month.

Semester Structure

Semester I: 8 compulsory papers. Semester II: 8 compulsory papers. Semester III: 2 compulsory papers plus 6 special papers of opted dual specialization, three from each specialization. Semester IV: 2 compulsory papers plus 4 special papers of opted dual specialization, two from each specialization. Project Study/Thesis is equivalent to 2 papers of 200 marks.

Career Opportunities

Management, administration, finance, marketing, HR, operations, entrepreneurship, and corporate roles; exact outcomes and placement numbers require admin-verified data.
