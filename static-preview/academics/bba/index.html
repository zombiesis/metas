Overview

The live BBA page states that the programme shall be called Bachelor of Business Administration (Honors), abbreviated BBA. GTU 2025-26 affiliation separately lists Bachelor of Business Administration (Honours / Honours with Research), 4-year duration, approved intake 120.

Duration

GTU 2025-26 affiliation lists 4 years for BBA Honours / Honours with Research; live legacy page text must be reconciled with current GTU rules before final publication.

Eligibility

Live page states plus 2 (10+2) from Arts and Commerce or equivalent recognized board; 60% aggregate through English medium; final list based on qualifying exam percentage and personal interview. Current GTU admission rules must be confirmed before publishing final eligibility.

Admission Process

Live page states admission final list is prepared based on qualifying examination marks and performance in the Personal Interview. Admin must reconcile this with current GTU admission process.

Attendance Rules

Minimum 75% attendance in each subject of each semester for end-semester examination eligibility. Attendance status of students with excess absences may be sent to parents for counselling.

Semester Structure

Live page states candidates may proceed to the next semester even if results/papers are pending, subject to university ordinances; failed papers must be cleared within permitted subsequent chances. Degree must be cleared within 5 academic years / 10 semesters from registration.

Career Opportunities

Business administration, marketing, finance, HR, operations, entrepreneurship, analytics, and further management studies; exact outcome claims require admin-verified data.
