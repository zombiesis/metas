Eligibility

10+2 with English and minimum 40% at the qualifying examination from a recognized board; candidates from recognized State Open School/NIOS are eligible though Science is preferable. Vocational ANM with 40%, vocational Health Care Science with 40%, and registered ANM are also listed. Foreign national equivalency must be obtained through Association of Indian Universities, New Delhi.

Admission Process

Admission once a year. For management quota, the live page lists advertisement by Admission Committee, PIN through Axis Bank against 12th mark sheet, registration at medadmgujarat.org, help centre reporting, original documentation/fee steps, reporting to Metas Adventist School of Nursing, and counselling by the Principal.
